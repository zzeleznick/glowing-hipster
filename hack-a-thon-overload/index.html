Hack-a-thon Overload

An itch I cannot scratch

HACK-A-THONS

There is no possible way that one can attend every hackathon, but the prospects of

  • building a working application in ~24 hours
  • instant feedback
  • immediate validation in the forms of awards,prizes, tech, swag
  • straight-up job-offers
  • verifiable resume items

    are certainly hard to resist.

    Look-out for

  • hackUCI May 23th-25th, Irvine CA

  • CodeDay Spring 2014 May 24th-25th, CA

  • API Con 2014 May 27th-29th, SF CA
