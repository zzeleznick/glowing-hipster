Hackahack

Oh Dear

I really want to attend the Intel-sponsored hack-a-thon this upcoming May 31st - June 1st; however, the timing is quite perplexing.

Rather than hosting the hackathon right after finals, hosting the hackathon at the end of May, makes a puzzling conundrum. While I have hesitated attending any more hackathons until finals were over, I intended to spend at least 2 weeks with my family in SoCal before heading back up to Berkeley in anticipation of start-up work.

I am extremely conflicted, as I really need to spend time with my family -- I have been a relatively ungrateful brood this past semester -- and I will be leaving for France to spend 6 weeks abroad before the next semester starts.

My finals end this upcoming Tuesday, May 13th.

Would leaving on May 30th be worthwhile?

Addicted to hackathons

And I really want new toys to play with, things to build, prizes to win...

Zachary Zeleznick
